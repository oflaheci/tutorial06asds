	\section*{Warner Brothers Report}
\begin{itemize}
	\item[] We found that Warner Brothers has not made a drama film since the beginning of this data collection. We also found that dramas receive a higher proportion of best picture nominations than any other genre.
	\item Our recommendation is to Warner Brothers to increase their production of drama films. 
	\item[] Nominated films that are dramas: 77 percent 
	\item[] Non-nominated films that are dramas: 46 percent 
	item[] Warner Bros films that are dramas: 0 percent
\end{itemize}

\begin{itemize}
	\item[] Through linear regression, we found that films that get nominated for best picture are on average 28 minutes longer than other films.
	\item We recommend Warner Brothers to make longer films!
\end{itemize}
